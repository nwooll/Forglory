About the Project

“CG” is online fantasy card game that can be player with one to two players. The user’s goal is to defeat their opponent using a pre-constructed deck of cards. The game will feature a colorfully illustrated cast of fully animated characters, a library of handdrawn cards and myriad of dynamic landscapes. Gameplay will include a single player vs AI, and two player hot seat mode. Currently the game is in early development.
